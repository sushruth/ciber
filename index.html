EYLEA® (afibercept solution for injection)

Once every 2 monthsa dosage regimen for treatment
of wet age-related macular degeneration (AMD)

Efficacy Summary of Product Characteristics Safety Information Administration Press Release

The recommended dose of EYLEA is 2 mg (equivalent to 50 microliters solution for injection).

EYLEA treatment is initiated with 1 injection per month (every 4 weeks) for 3 consecutive doses, followed by 1 injection every 2 months (8 weeks). There is no requirement for monitoring between injections.

Long term (after the first 12 months of treatment), it is recommended that patients continue to be treated with EYLEA every 2 months (8 weeks). However, after the first 12 months of treatment with EYLEA, the treatment interval could be extended up to every 3 months (12 weeks) based on visual and anatomic outcomes.

EYLEA may be dosed as frequently as once per month (4 weeks).
